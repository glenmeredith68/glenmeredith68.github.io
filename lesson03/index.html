Site Name

The name of the site is Fishing in Medway. The domain name is fishinginmedway.net which plays up on the fishing theme by using a .net ending. According to GoDaddy it costs $12 per year.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information to help fishers in the Medway, MA area and to increase desire and excitement to fish in the area. The site will provide services such as showing the weather for the town as well as several surrounding towns. It will also outline on the map some of the prime spots for fishing. There will be a page for tips so that those new to the hobby can sharpen their knowledge and there will be a gallery to help everyone get excited to catch some fish!
